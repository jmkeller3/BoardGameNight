Welcome to Board Game Night Finder

Interested in Board Games but have few friends to play with? Want to experience a fun night out playing advanced strategies games? Or do you have a large Board Game library and want to share your games with others? This website is for you!

What We Do

Our algorithms take only a few pieces of user information in order to identify the closest board game events nearest to you. First, when prompted, you will need to authorize our website to accessess your geolocation. Second, you will be redirected to authorize basic access to a Meetup.com account. It will return you back with the closest board game events near you!

Results

Below are the results for your area. Check out the descriptions behind the cards. If there are none that interest you, start your own group at Meetup.com!

  • A Place for Strangers to Connect

    Meetup.com is a place where thousands of people create and host events every week for people to gather and meet over a variety of topics. One such category is board games.

  • Why Board Game Night?

    The idea of board game night has familial roots, often limited to a few classics games created over fourty years ago. However, the tabletop scene has exploded with dozens of excellent new board games released over the past decade. Board game nights today offer people to learn, laugh, and love new games and make new friends.

  • How Does It Work?

    By inputing your location, we can identify the closest board game events posted on Meetup.com. We will post all the nearest locations on a Google Map and have the event details listed individually below.
